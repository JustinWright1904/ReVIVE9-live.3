REFUND & RETURN POLICY

Quick Reference

Contact: ShopReVIVE9@gmail.com

Returns Accepted Within: Five (5) days of delivery

Restocking Fee: 10%

Return Shipping: Buyer pays (except damaged/incorrect items)

Returns

If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, you must contact us within five (5) days of receiving your order to initiate a return.

After the 5-day return window, no returns or exchanges will be accepted.

A 10% restocking fee will be applied to all eligible returns, and the remaining balance will be issued as store credit only.

Returned items must be unworn, unaltered, and include all original tags and packaging. Items that do not meet these criteria will be denied and returned to the sender.

You will be notified once your return has been received and processed.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Clearance / Sale / Discounted items
  • Personalized items

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, you must contact us within three (3) days of delivery and provide clear photos of the issue.

For approved damage or incorrect-item claims, ReVIVE9 will provide a pre-paid return shipping label.

All items must be returned in original condition (unworn, unaltered) with packaging and tags intact.

Item(s) Missing from Order

Contact: ShopReVIVE9@gmail.com

Include:

  • A copy of your original invoice
  • A photo of what you received

For incorrect items, ReVIVE9 will provide a pre-paid label after verification.
